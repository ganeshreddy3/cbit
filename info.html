Attendance Guidelines

The Social Welfare Department, Govt of [State], has issued revised guidelines to colleges for the renewal of scholarships for the year [Upcoming Academic Year]. Accordingly, every scholarship holder shall maintain a minimum of **75% attendance** in the previous year [Current Academic Year].

The Heads of Departments are requested to certify the attendance of scholarship holders of their respective departments in the prescribed format for the year [Current Academic Year]. The concerned student must enclose the certificate issued by the Head of the Department along with a hard copy of the Scholarship Online Renewal Application Form and submit it in the scholarship section for further processing.
